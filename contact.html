Before You Visit

We recommend booking your appointment at least 3-5 days in advance, especially during weekends and holiday seasons. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability. Please bring your puppy's vaccination records on your first visit. If your puppy has any special needs, allergies, or behavioural quirks, let us know in advance so we can prepare the perfect experience. We also offer a "pre-visit" tour if your puppy is nervous — just ask!

We're a fear-free facility, so we never use harsh restraints or punish bad behaviour. We use positive reinforcement, treats, and gentle handling. Our goal is to make grooming a happy, relaxing ritual for your puppy, not a stressful chore.

Why We Love Connecting With You

At The Floof Lab, we believe that every interaction is an opportunity to build a lasting relationship. Whether you're a first-time puppy parent or a seasoned dog lover, we're here to support you with expert advice, compassionate care, and a genuine love for animals. Our team is passionate about what we do, and we're always excited to meet new furry friends and their humans.

We understand that leaving your puppy with a groomer can be a big deal — that's why we go above and beyond to earn your trust. From the moment you walk in, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and a calming environment. We'll take the time to understand your puppy's unique personality, preferences, and any concerns you may have. We believe in transparent communication, so we'll always keep you informed about what we're doing and why.

We also love giving back to the community. We host quarterly "Puppy Playdates" and "Grooming 101" workshops where you can learn tips and tricks for maintaining your puppy's coat at home. These events are free and open to all — check our social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated. We also partner with local shelters and rescue organizations to offer discounted grooming for adopted dogs.

When you contact us, you're not just reaching out to a business — you're connecting with a team that truly cares. We read every message, every review, and every piece of feedback. Your voice helps us improve and grow. So don't be shy — reach out with questions, ideas, or even just to share a cute photo of your puppy. We'd love to see it!

We're also proud to be a pet-friendly workplace. Our team members bring their own dogs to work (with proper introductions, of course), creating a joyful, relaxed atmosphere that your puppy will feel the moment they step through the door. We believe that happy humans + happy puppies = the best grooming experience possible.

Thank you for considering The Floof Lab. We can't wait to meet you and your furry best friend. Whether you're booking a full spa day, looking for a quick nail trim, or just want to chat about puppy care, we're here for you. Drop us a line, give us a call, or stop by — we always have treats ready (for both you and your pup!).
